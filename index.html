Cat

Cats are the most adorable pet. They are not super pushy but then again not too rude.
I would say they have their own personality

  • Scientafic Name:Felis catus
  • Class:Mammalia
  • Suborder:Feliformia
  • Family:Felidae

They are kept by humans for hunting rodents and as companions.There are also farm cats, which are kept on farms to keep rodents away; and feral cats, which are domestic cats that live away from humans. A cat is sometimes called a kitty.
